Focusing on a locally AdS subclass that remains well-defined in the zero-coupling limit, they recover the standard C-metric and identify two new representations of AdS spacetime. The significance lies in demonstrating how geometry influences soliton confinement, existence, and structure, with exact solutions provided in Minkowski, Schwarzschild, and de Sitter spacetimes.|

|**2026-07-16**|**An exactly solvable macroscopic fluctuation theory of single-file diffusion**|Sandeep Jangid, Soumyabrata Saha, Kapil Sharma, Jitendra Kethepalli, Benjamin Guiselin, Jacopo De Nardis, Tridib Sadhu|[[arxiv:2607.14073](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.14073)]|This paper solves the macroscopic fluctuation theory (MFT) for single-file diffusion of extended Brownian hard rods (BHR), a problem previously considered intractable. The method employs a dynamical formulation of stationary scattering, mapping the problem to an effective non-Hermitian Hamiltonian. Key results include explicit analytic expressions for diffracted beam amplitudes that are valid beyond the paraxial approximation. The work is significant for providing new, exact analytic solutions for electromagnetic wave scattering by complex gratings, applicable to both TE and TM polarizations.|

This study provides a unified mechanism linking the VHS and antiferromagnetic nesting to both anomalous metallic and insulating behaviors in cuprates.|
